About JumpToWork

Jump To Work is a network of students, higher education and companies with a wide range of global partners. With our help, creating reliable and strong connections for educational, commercial purposes and advancing in career is no longer challenging.

Our Services

We provide services for Student, Company, and University

STUDENTS

Students can search and apply for different job positions in our JobBoard. Thesis work possibility is available too. Jump To Work also provides School Directory which has information about higher educational institutions around the world.

COMPANIES

Jump To Work assists companies’ recruitment process by creating opportunities to advertise job positions. Having access to our CV bank and reaching the best talents are undeniably beneficial. Collaboration with global schools is also worth-trying

SCHOOLS

Schools are enabled to introduce job positions in our JobBoard to their students. Our School Directory develops schools’ abilities to market themselves to prospective students globally. Connecting with international companies for further cooperation is another enormous benefit as well
